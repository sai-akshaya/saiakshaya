shastra junior quiz


Shaastra Junior Quiz, although introduced just last year,
began with a bang, becoming one of the most popular events
at Shaastra. This year we aim to make it even bigger and more
wide-spread, by conducting it in all major cities in India.
The finalists will battle it out in the grand finale at IIT-M
during Shaastra to win several goodies and cash prizes,
along with the honour of claiming the title of Shaastra
Junior Quiz champions.
This quiz requires more quick thinking and basic general knowledge
than an extensive encyclopedic memory. Many of the questions can be
worked out from the clues in them, and promise to be interesting and
approachable. With this quiz, we aim to improve the interest in, and
level of, the quizzes at school-level. We also hope to continue
building further after the success of the previous edition.



The quiz is being held in multiple cities across the country.
Each city follows a set pattern. A written prelims open to everyone,
of which 6 best teams (of 2 members each) from the city vie it out for
a place in the national quarters in Shaastra. At the national quarters,
the best of the pack will emerge through the stage rounds during Shaastra
and win the title of Shaastra Junior Quiz champions.



1. What is the age restriction for participation, if any?
A)There is no age restriction, and we encourage every student to give it
their best shot. The level of the questions is mostly aimed
at 10th, 11th and 12th standard students.
2. What topics does the quiz cover?
A)The quiz will be general in nature, covering everything under the
sun. Slightly more importance will be given to the sci-tech genre,
and teams can expect a sizable amount of questions from here.
3. How do I register for this event?
A)The quiz follows an on spot registration pattern, and teams can just
show up at the venue with their school id.
The Shaastra facebook page will have venue details and timings.
4. My city regional is over, but I REALLY want to participate. What do I do?
A)TRAVEL. Travel to your nearest city, and give it a shot. Passion,
determination and intelligence is what separates a winner from the pack.

Feel free to mail your doubts to sjq@shaastra.org
